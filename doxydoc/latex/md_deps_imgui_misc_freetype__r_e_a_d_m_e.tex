\hypertarget{md_deps_imgui_misc_freetype__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md70}{}\doxysubsection{Gamma Correct Blending}\label{md_deps_imgui_misc_freetype__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md70}
Free\+Type assumes blending in linear space rather than gamma space. See Free\+Type note for \href{https://www.freetype.org/freetype2/docs/reference/ft2-base_interface.html\#FT_Render_Glyph}{\texttt{ F\+T\+\_\+\+Render\+\_\+\+Glyph}}. For correct results you need to be using s\+R\+GB and convert to linear space in the pixel shader output.
